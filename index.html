Shih-Yi Chien

Welcome! I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Management Information Systems at National Chengchi University. I completed my Ph.D. in the School of Computing and Information at the University of Pittsburgh, and was an adjunct faculty in this founding iSchool. My background spans the field of Human-Automation Collaboration, including UX, Robotics and Cognitive Science. My current research projects examine Interaction Quality of Human-Humanoid Robot Collaboration, Trust and Privacy in Human-Automation Interaction Across Cultures, and Brain Dynamics (EEG) in Decision-Making Process. Welcome any opportunities for future collaboration! My previous research projects are listed below; for more information, please go to my research page or see my publications.
